Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects only the information you voluntarily provide, such as expense amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates. No payment or banking details are stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to power the expense tracking features of Spendly — including generating summaries, category breakdowns, and date-based filters. We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored securely in our database. We employ standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated statistics with trusted service providers solely to operate and improve the application.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@spendly.app. We will make every effort to respond promptly and resolve any issues.
